First Aid for a person choking

First, encourage the person to cough. Second, bend the person forwards and give up to 5 back blows to try and dislodge the blockage. If he/she is still choking, give up to 5 abdominal thrusts: hold around the waist and pull inwards and upwards above the belly button.

First Aid for a bleeding wound

Apply firm pressure over the wound. Use a sterile or clean bulky pad and apply it firmly with hand pressure. Apply a bandage to keep the dressing in place.

First Aid for an unconscious person

Call or tell someone to call 911 or the local emergency number. Check the person's airway, breathing, and pulse frequently. If necessary, begin CPR. If the person is breathing and lying on their back, and you do not think there is a spinal injury, carefully roll the person toward you onto their side
